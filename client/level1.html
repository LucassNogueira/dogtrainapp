Training Level 1


Training should be done in sessions no longer than 25 min untill level 4. Completing commands in one session does not warrent a level up, spend at minium 10 sessions on each level. Aim for 2 sessions per day minium. Click any command card to see a description of command!

For “come,” simply put your dog on a leash and say the command while you tug lightly on their leash. When he or she comes to you, reward them with a treat. This direction will take consecutive days of training, so be patient.

To teach your dog “sit”, hold a treat in your fist and put it above your dogs head. Slowly move it behind their head and when they crouch, say “sit” and reward them.

Place a treat in your hand and make a tight fist and hold it at nose level. Before your dog has lifted their paw, say “High Five!.” The moment they lift their paw and touch your hand, mark it with a “Yes!” then reward them out of your other hand.
