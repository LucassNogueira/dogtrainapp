Howdy yall! here at PogDog we like to focus on obedience training while building a strong bond between you and your pup. When training, if your dog begins to become frustrated or resistant to commands. Its best to take a break and let your dog rest for a bit, the mental stimulation from training will tire them out more than physical exertion! Remeber obedience training is not something that will come overnight, these commands must be worked on throughout your pups life to build a good foundation. A solid approach to training is to pre-portion your dogs daily food into 2 ziplock bags, and use their food as treats for training. This way you are not feeding them all at once, rather a little throughout the day untill they finish both bags.
