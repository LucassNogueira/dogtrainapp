Training Level 4


Welcome to level 4 training! You and your pup sure have come a long way. Sessions can now range between 30-45 min, but it will depend on the temperment of your dog. Keep sessions at 3 per day max, remeber to click the cards for a description of the commands youre working on!

“roll” is taught by having your dog in the “down” position. Then with a treat, have your dog roll onto their side. Move the treat to the side of their head, all the while encouraging them as they lay down. Then, continue to move the treat to the side while saying “Roll over.”

Using a treat in your fist, guide your dog around in a circular motion. As he makes a full turn, say "spin" and reward.

Hold a strong-smelling treat in front of your pup and wait for them to smell it. Once they do, bring the treat down to the floor and your dog will eventually follow. When their belly is on the floor say “down” and give them the treat.
